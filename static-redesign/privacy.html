Twitodon privacy policy

This service collects your Twitter ID and your Mastodon username and server domain name. This is then saved in the database providing a link between your Twitter identity and your Mastodon identity. No other information is saved.

When a user logs into Twitodon and downloads the offered CSV file they are receiving a correlated map of Twitter IDs of users they follow matched against those users’ Mastodon usernames and server domain names. If you do not want to be discoverable on Mastodon by users who follow you on Twitter, then do not login and use this service.

Twitodon does not utilise any analytics or advertising cookies.
